F.A.Q

  • What is the Cyboss Cocreation Event?

    This Cyboss Cocreation Events are usually conducted by Cyboss itself to invite those Merchants who can offer a better REWARDS to our Consumer Marchants and Resellers to join in as our Products Supplier or Wholeseller to our Franchisees and Chainstores who can sell the products to their customers. Others than this, our Cyboss qualified Trainers (AG) and Consultants (SM3) can conduct themself to bring in more Merchants and Suppliers.

  • How to become the Cyboss qualified Trainers and Consultants?

    All Cyboss qualified Trainers and Consultants are verified by Cyboss Cocreation after special Training Courses and SELECTED to apply for those post and seek for verification from their Train The Trainer's Guru before comes to verify by Cyboss Cocreation to earn their Cyboss CPR rewards from every Products that sold to customers.

  • What is the Cyboss CPR rewards?

    The full explanation of CPR is Cocreation Product Reseller which was specially designed for those qualified AG Trainers and SM3 Consultants to earn the REWARDS which are given from the Franchisees and Chainstores seller directly to those qualified AG Trainers and SM3 Consultants from their hardwork to bring them in to Cyboss Cocreating Market Place.

  • What type of Business can JOIN in to become the Merchants in Cyboss Cocreation Platform?

    Cyboss Cocreation apply the Horizontal Alliance Business Model's operation under a unique requirement to allow One line of Business in One Zipcode Zone to operate their Main Products Supplier OR selling in that specific approved Zone OR Territory.

  • What are the Conclusion made by Cyboss Cocreation when approached the same line of business WHO want to sell in the same Zone?

    This will be considered as Waiting List IF the former Merchant who offen the Law of Agreement signed OR they want to resign the Contract by themself after SEVEN working days, then the new applicants may apply for Verification and Approved from Cyboss Cocreation to operate and sell their specific Products in the same Zone.

  • What is the Law of TWO Excuses Theory application in Cyboss Cocreation?

    All Merchants need to aware of this TWO Excuses Theory which are applied STRICTLY and effective upon signing up the Agreement infront of Witness between the Merchants and Cyboss Cocreation. This will be binded in the said Agreement that there will be NO Excuses After the Merchant offen the Agreement Clause of NOT TO DO happened TWICE. The FIRST time will be EXCUSED because of NOT SURE which are stated in the said Agreement and the Second time Happen AGAIN which are EXCUSED because of FORGOT the rules stated in the said Agreement. BUT if it happen again in the third time, there will be NO EXCUSE which may happen to STOP the operation immediately and FINED. For more detail, please read carefully before you apply as our Merchants.
